Next release in the works

04/25/18

Our developers have been hard at work for a few months now, and we are finally going to give you some information on our new game, Explorer. "This game will be our most ambitious project yet" says lead developer Ethan Temprovich, ". . .you will be able to traverse an almost endless galaxy filled with star systems, space pirates and much more." As Ethan said, you will be able to explore a randomly generated galaxy all to yourself, or a friend! Yes there will be local co-op up to 4 players to help explore your star systems.

Furthermore, it was designed behind our Java Game Engine, STL; meaning we will release it on both Windows and Mac. Here is an early screenshot:

Also, all of the data for the objects in the game is loaded from our custom object notation language, TN. This means that every galaxy, star system, star, and planet can be 100% unique. Here is an example of a file:
