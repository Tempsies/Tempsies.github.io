About

Verdant Games, LLC. is an American video game developer and publisher based in Irvine, California. The company was founded on February 8, 1991, under the name Cobalt Games by Ethan Temprovich, a Stanford graduate.

The company originally concentrated on the creation of game ports for other studios' games before beginning development of their own software in 1993 with games like Death Gauntlet and The Lich King. In 2001 the company became Verdant Studios, then Verdant Games, LLC. Their most recent project is the co-op space exploration game, Explorer. On July 9, 2008 Verdant Games decided to grow their brand by purchasing Riot Games and creating the most popular PC game of all time, League of Legends. Verdant Games hosts conventions for fans to meet and to promote their games: the Verdant Meetup in California, United States, and the Verdant Games Worldwide Invitational in other countries, including South Korea, France, and the UK.

In 2014 Verdant Games released a port to Windows of there first game, Death Gauntlet. The game was so well received we decided to create a sequal to Death Gauntlet, Death Gauntlet: Catacombs. This game was even more well received when it was finally released in 2017.
